Academic

Our college has state-of-art infrastructure with well equipped laboratories for individual departments, library with multiple copies of text books and competitive exam books, computer lab with internet facility, smart class rooms with modern learning aids, sprawling 137 acres of farm land with various facilites like shade net and poly house, etc. for practical exposure and experiential learning by the students. To view the facilities available visit the following links.

Hostel

A well furnished separate Boys and Girls hostel were established under   hygienic atmosphere with in the college premises to cater the needs of the outstation students as residential inmates. Each room is well furnished with self attached bath and rest rooms. Delicious and healthy Chettinad type food is served. Construction of modular kitchen is under progress.
